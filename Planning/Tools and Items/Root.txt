Name: Root
Looks: Blue (#4084c4) and a black circle in the center

WHAT IT DOES:
Holds plant samples. Once a sample is collected, it will be put in the database.
At any time, you can access it and it will show what you have collected.
You will be able to also go into a specific one and it will show it's data. This data includes
-Poisonous levels and effects
-Area found
-Scientific name
-Name you have chosen

It will also say how many plant samples you have out of all of them. (Ex. 3/6)
If a plant swab is taken, it will show if it is poisonous or not.
